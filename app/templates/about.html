What is LearnBot?

LearnBot is an AI-powered educational assistant that uses IBM watsonx.ai and the Granite 3.0 8B Instruct model to transform complex academic content into clear, level-appropriate explanations.

Using a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline, LearnBot indexes your uploaded documents and retrieves the most relevant passages before generating responses — ensuring answers are grounded in your actual course material.
